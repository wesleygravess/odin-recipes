Cashew Pesto Sauce

Home

Description

Creamy, vibrant, and dairy-free, this cashew pesto brings a silky twist to the classic basil sauce. Blended with soaked cashews, fresh basil, garlic, and lemon, it’s packed with nutty richness and herby brightness. Perfect tossed with pasta, spread on sandwiches, or drizzled over roasted vegetables — it’s an incredibly versatile sauce that tastes indulgent but is totally plant-based.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw cashews (soaked 2+ hours or boiled 10 min)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • ¼ cup nutritional yeast (or Parmesan)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • Water as needed (2–4 tbsp for thinning)

Steps:

  1. Prep cashews:
    • If you didn’t soak them overnight, boil them for 10 minutes and drain.

  2. Blend:
    • In a food processor or blender, combine soaked cashews, basil, garlic, lemon juice, nutritional yeast, and salt.
    • Pulse until finely chopped.
    • With the motor running, slowly add olive oil and blend until smooth.

  3. Adjust:
    • Add 2–4 tbsp water to thin to desired consistency. Taste and adjust salt or lemon if needed.

  4. Serve or store:
    • Use immediately or store in the fridge up to 5 days. Freeze for longer storage.
